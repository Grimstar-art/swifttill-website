COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about SwiftTill POS and how it can help your business

How long does installation take?

Installation and setup typically takes 2-4 hours depending on your store setup and the number of products in your inventory. Our team handles everything, and you'll receive comprehensive on-site training as part of the package.

Is there any monthly or recurring fee?

No! SwiftTill is a one-time payment of Ksh 20,000 for the complete software package. There are no monthly subscriptions, no hidden charges. You get free software updates for 1 year and 6 months of technical support included.

Can I use SwiftTill on multiple devices?

Absolutely! SwiftTill POS is designed for multi-user access. You can have multiple terminals in your store all syncing in real-time. Plus, with our cloud sync feature, you can manage multiple locations from anywhere.

What payment methods does SwiftTill support?

SwiftTill supports M-Pesa payments, card payments, cash, cheques, and custom payment methods. Automatic reconciliation means your finances are always up to date. Integration with QuickBooks makes accounting seamless.

What happens if I lose internet connection?

SwiftTill works offline! You can continue processing sales, and data automatically syncs when your connection is restored. This ensures your business never stops, even during network outages.

Do you provide training and support?

Yes! Training is included during installation. We also provide 6 months of free technical support, video tutorials, documentation, and our support team is available Monday-Saturday. Extended support packages are available.

Is my business data secure?

Security is our top priority. We use bank-grade encryption, PCI DSS compliance, automatic daily backups, and role-based access controls. Your data is protected with industry-leading security standards.
